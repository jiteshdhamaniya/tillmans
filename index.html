Why We Prefer Full-Spectrum Hemp Extract

There are essentially two different philosophies when it comes to CBD oils — they can be made from CBD isolate or a full-spectrum hemp extract.

We chose full-spectrum extract because they hit harder, last longer, and cover a wider range of benefits.

Our full-spectrum extracts maintain all the cannabinoids and terpenes naturally produced by the hemp plant. This includes CBD, CBG, CBC, THCV, and much more. All of these cannabinoids have been shown to work together to produce a stronger level of benefit overall. This is a concept known as the entourage effect.
